# Case Study: Blavity Portfolio

## Summary

At Blavity, I lead product, engineering, and design strategy across a multi-brand portfolio spanning B2B SaaS and D2C mobile products.

## Product areas

- Talent Infusion
- AfroTech Connect
- Travel Noire

## Strategic focus

- Shared architecture across brands
- Reusable design systems
- AI-native product development workflows
- Consumer mobile engagement and retention
- Enterprise SaaS activation and monetization
- Portfolio-level roadmap prioritization

## Representative work

### Talent Infusion

Launched and scaled a B2B SaaS product focused on employer adoption, onboarding, activation, and paid upgrades.

### AfroTech Connect

Scaled a consumer mobile experience around high-traffic event moments, community engagement, and post-event retention.

### Travel Noire

Designed and launched a consumer mobile product extending the brand into app-based membership, destination discovery, and trip planning workflows.

## Operating model

I work across strategy, design, implementation, and leadership. That includes creating Figma systems, shaping roadmap priorities, aligning stakeholders, and using AI-native development workflows to accelerate delivery.
