## Operating model

I operate across the full product loop:

1. Understand the customer and market
2. Define the product bet
3. Design the experience
4. Align stakeholders
5. Build or guide implementation
6. Launch, measure, and iterate

My recent work has focused heavily on AI-native workflows that reduce handoffs between product, design, and engineering while preserving strategic clarity and product quality.
