# Case Study: Outwork

## Summary

Outwork is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for campaign and volunteer management. I built and launched it end-to-end as a solo founder and product builder.

## Role

Solo founder, product strategist, designer, and builder.

## Problem

Political and civic organizations often rely on disconnected tools for volunteer management, donations, events, compliance, and outreach. This creates duplicated work, weak supporter context, and slow operational execution.

## Product approach

Outwork was designed as an integrated organizing platform with AI-assisted workflows to reduce manual work and help organizers focus on relationships.

## Key capabilities

- Volunteer CRM
- Contact logs
- Events and attendance
- Donations
- Vendors and expenditures
- Stripe billing
- Role-based access control
- Real-time data sync
- AI summaries
- Suggested action items
- OCR-assisted data entry

## Product decisions

- Multi-tenant architecture from day one
- AI used for summarization, extraction, and recommendations
- Human review required for AI-assisted data entry
- Modular UI patterns reused across product areas
- Compliance-aware workflows without building a full accounting system

## Outcome

Built and launched in under 90 days and signed the first paying customer within three months.
