# Case Study: Workday Credentials

## Summary

Workday Credentials was a verifiable credentials product that included APIs, web applications, and a digital wallet.

## Role

Founding product manager.

## Product challenge

Verifiable credentials required coordination across product, engineering, legal, security, marketing, and sales. The product needed to support enterprise-grade trust while helping customers understand a new category.

## Product scope

- APIs
- Web applications
- Digital wallet
- Enterprise adoption workflows
- Compliance and security alignment

## Product lessons

- Category-creating products require as much education as feature delivery
- Security and legal constraints should shape the roadmap early
- Platform products need clear developer, buyer, and end-user value propositions
