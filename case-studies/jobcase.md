# Case Study: Jobcase Employer Products

## Summary

At Jobcase, I led product work across employer-facing products, including Employer Center and Hiring Events.

## Problem

Employers needed better tools to activate, manage, and scale hiring workflows within a marketplace environment.

## Product focus

- Employer activation
- Enterprise acquisition funnel
- Hiring events
- Repeatable revenue streams
- Roadmap coordination across multiple engineering teams

## Role

Director of Product, coordinating roadmap execution across five engineering teams and three designers while partnering with Sales and Customer Success leadership.

## Key outcomes

- Launched Employer Center
- Improved employer activation
- Built Hiring Events into a repeatable revenue stream
- Increased adoption through enterprise funnel optimization

## Product lessons

- Activation requires both product clarity and GTM alignment
- Marketplace products need tight feedback loops between sales, customer success, and product
- Repeatable workflows often matter more than one-off feature depth
